Copy and Design Brief for Mid-Year Sale VIP List Building

Below is the brief for each popup, the copy for every step, and the KV assets Marco has prepared. Everything Alia's team needs to design and build.

The two popups

What we need built.

Popup 1 · Full-page

Triggered on a dedicated URL

Acts like a landing page on top of the regular homepage. Used for paid traffic, email and SMS landing pages driving traffic into the campaign.

URL
boncharge.com/?mys (the ?mys query param triggers the popup directly on the homepage; no redirect or custom page needed)
Behind it
The regular BON CHARGE homepage. No custom landing page to build.
Audience
Visible to everyone landing on the URL. No suppression.
Style
Full-page takeover. Treat as landing-page-grade design.
Popup 2 · BAU takeover

Replaces the current site-wide popup

Standard BAU popup behaviour across the site. Runs for the duration of the list-build campaign, then reverts to the previous BAU popup when the sale opens.

Trigger
Match current BAU rules (delay or exit-intent).
Audience
New visitors and non-subscribers only. Existing subs excluded, reached via email instead.
Suppression
Standard cookie suppression after submit or dismiss.
Reverts
On Mid-Year Sale open day, BAU popup reverts to the previous version.
