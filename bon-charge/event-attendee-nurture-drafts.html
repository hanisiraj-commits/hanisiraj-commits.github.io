The light
Red Light (660nm) and Near-Infrared (850nm) are specific wavelengths of light your body responds to. When your skin and cells absorb them, they are designed to support the way your cells make their own energy. More cellular energy is the groundwork for how your body repairs, recovers and renews itself. It is light doing real work, not warmth you simply feel.

The warmth
Alongside the light, gentle infrared heat helps your body ease into its natural rest and recovery response. This is the part you feel most in the moment, the loosening, the calm, the sense of your system settling. It is the same response your body reaches for after good movement or deep rest.

The result
Put the two together and you get why a proper session tends to leave people feeling restored rather than wired. It is not a quick hit of energy. It is your body being supported to do what it already knows how to do, a little better, the more consistently you use it.
