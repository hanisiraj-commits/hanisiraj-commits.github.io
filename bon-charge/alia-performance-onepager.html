View 1 · Before Alia vs After Alia · Matched 25% sales

Before Alia: Recharge Sale. After Alia: Memorial Day Sale.

View 2 · Before Alia vs After Alia · BAU (no promo)

BAU without Alia → BAU with Alia.

Why BAU shows a bigger lift than Sale-vs-Sale

On a sale day, traffic is already high-intent — the 25% discount itself converts a large share of visitors regardless of any onsite tooling. There's less headroom for Alia to add value. On a BAU day, intent is more mixed, and Alia's quizzes, full-page flows, and targeted captures have far more room to redirect lower-intent visitors into the funnel. Both effects are real — the BAU lift is the truer "Alia effect" on a normal trading day.
