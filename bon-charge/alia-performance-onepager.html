Note on Welcome Series attribution

The 2025 Welcome Series flow (Y4NHS7) shows $211K attributed revenue in the after window vs $327K before — most of the 11K Alia-acquired subscribers are still mid-flow at the end of the reporting window. Welcome flow uplift from this cohort is a lagging indicator and will land in the next 30–60 days. We've also flagged a check on whether Alia subscribers are entering the existing Welcome Series trigger (Added to List) or land on a different list — material to lock in before the next reporting cut.
