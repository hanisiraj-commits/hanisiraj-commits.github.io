"Awareness → Memory → Education → Identity → Purchase"
8 emails 4 branches 20 copy columns ~18-21 days

A nurture flow for people who physically experienced BON CHARGE at an in-person event — recovery lounges, wellness activations, fitness partnerships (Karve Club red light yoga), pop-ups, spa and creator events. They have felt the tech and met the brand, but have not built product belief. Not cold, not ready-to-buy: warm awareness-stage leads with high latent intent if we warm them correctly. The flow reconnects the memory, teaches the science, builds belief, ties the brand to identity, then converts with a time-boxed attendee offer.

05Refinements to the briefflagged before build
Consent must be an unticked explicit opt-in, not pre-ticked
The brief specifies both "pre-ticked" and "explicit marketing consent" — these conflict, and a pre-ticked box isn't GDPR-valid (BON CHARGE ships EU). Correct to an unticked, explicit opt-in before Lukas pushes the waiver live.
Discount caps at 20%, not 25%
The brief proposes "20-25%". 25% is the sale ceiling and can't appear in a non-sale flow. Event offer = 20% max, one code, no stacking language. Open question: align to the 15% new-customer standard, or run a distinct 20% event rate? Recommend 20% — event exclusivity justifies it.
Waiver goal options should mirror the 4 pillars
Recommend Skin · Sleep · Recovery · General wellness (rather than 5 loosely-mapped interests), so the event_goal property stays consistent with every other BON CHARGE flow. Recommended fields: Name · Email · Goal · "Used BON CHARGE before?" · explicit consent. The "used before?" answer feeds the entry filter, not a second split.
EU withdrawal block is N/A here
This is a pre-purchase flow — no purchase happens inside it — so the 14-day withdrawal block used in post-purchase flows doesn't apply. Sender stays no-reply@boncharge.com, from-name BON CHARGE.
