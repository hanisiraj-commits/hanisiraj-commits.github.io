Note
Solene is the proven hero
Two Solene-led emails generated $3,132, which is 37% of April’s revenue from 22% of the sends. The dress is doing the heavy lifting and the May plan continues to feature it.
Note
Elena letters drive the most orders
The Easter Last Chance Elena Letter produced 19 orders, the highest single-email order count of the month. The plain-text founder format converts when a sale is closing.
Note
Plain text is winning click rate right now
The two highest click rates of April both came from plain-text founder letters (0.34% and 0.60%). The image-led editorial sends are landing opens but not clicks. We’re continuing to test this.
Note
Narrow segments lift click rate and RPR
The "#1 Best-seller" send hit 6,474 people instead of the usual 18-21k and produced $0.15 RPR, two to three times the broad-send average. Tighter audiences are pulling more revenue per send.
Watch
Click rate is the bottleneck
Open rate is healthy at 56% on average, but click rate sits at 0.28%. The audience is engaged with the brand, just not converting through the email itself. We’re testing format and CTA mechanics across May to lift this.
Watch
Mother’s Day, still open
Apr 24 launch reached 36,318 (the largest blast of the month) and the Apr 30 Elena Close is queued. Early click rate is soft at 0.15% but the 5-day attribution window doesn’t close until 29 Apr. Three more touches across May 5-10 will compound the read before the final number is in.

Campaigns
The Plan
Nine sends across May. Each one grouped by week with its objective, products, promo requirements, and the April insight that informed it.
Week One Mother’s Day Close
01
The Mother’s Day Edit, 5 Days to Go
Mother’s Day · Tuesday 5 May · Image Editorial
A curated dress gift guide. Five dresses styled for different mum archetypes. The brunch mum, the holiday mum, the at-home mum, the busy mum, and one for yourself. Time-poor gifters who haven’t bought yet are the main target.
Solene (11 colours) · Calypso Tiered · Odette · Riviera Floral Maxi · Verona Two-piece
15% off dresses · code MOM15 · ends 10 May
Why now: Same five dresses featured in the Apr 24 launch, so consistency reinforces the offer. Five days from Mother’s Day is the prime "I haven’t bought a gift yet" window. Send to engaged segments only, not the full database.
02
Mother’s Day Last Chance
Elena Letter · Sunday 10 May · Plain Text
Plain text founder letter. Sale ends midnight tonight. Personal urgency: "I just genuinely don’t want you to miss out." Names two or three specific dresses in specific colourways with a hyperlinked text CTA. Same template that drove 19 orders for Easter.
Solene in Oatstone · Calypso in Onyx · Odette as Elena’s pick
15% off dresses · code MOM15 · ends 10 May 11:59pm PT
Why now: Easter Last Chance Elena Letter generated the most orders of any April send (19). Send Sunday morning so it lands the morning of Mother’s Day, perfect timing for the treat-yourself segment.
Week Two Game Day Social Proof + New Arrivals Launch
03
Why Women Keep Coming Back to Game Day
Social Proof · Wednesday 13 May · Image Editorial
Same template that pulled $1,716 for the April Solene send, this time on the Game Day Set. Image-led with customer quotes embedded as image cards, single-product focus, four PDP links. The angle: built-in shorts, 15 colours, the set that earns its place in the rotation.
Game Day Set ($58, 15 colours) · Cross-sell: Cabin Cuff Socks
Why now: The Solene "Why Women Keep Reordering" template was the top revenue email of April. Switching the product to Game Day Set keeps the format fresh while running the same play that’s already proven. Game Day is the brand’s current bestseller and hasn’t had its own social proof moment yet.
04
New In: Three Just Landed
NPD Launch · Saturday 16 May · Image Editorial
One consolidated launch for the three new arrivals. Image-led with one stack per product (hero shot, colour grid, quick lifestyle context). Each product gets its own block but they share the email. Drives traffic to the new arrivals collection page as the umbrella destination.
Elara Pinafore ($58, jumper dress, 6 colours, S to 3XL) · Ease Two-Piece ($68, ribbed loungewear set, 6 colours, S to XL) · Clara Sculpt Bodysuit ($48, ribbed foundational layer, 9 colours, S to 2XL)
Why now: One email, three products. Avoids the multi-product roundup trap by giving each piece its own dedicated block instead of a blended "build a wardrobe" pitch. Tests which of the three pulls hardest before committing follow-up real estate.
Week Three Clara Spotlight + Memorial Day Open
05
Meet Clara, The Foundational Layer
NPD Spotlight · Tuesday 19 May · Image Editorial
Single-product feature for the Clara Sculpt Bodysuit. The foundational layer angle: ribbed, square neckline, smoothing, opaque, layers under everything. Picks up on the launch email’s standout if performance data comes in clearly, or runs anyway because the layering story doesn’t fit inside a multi-product launch.
Clara Sculpt Bodysuit ($48, 9 colours, S to 2XL) · Cross-sell: Loop Wrap or Cloud Modal Set
Why now: Bodysuits convert best when shown solo with clear positioning. Three days from launch is the right window to drill into the standout piece while it’s still warm.
06
Memorial Day Sale Launch
Elena Letter · Friday 22 May · Plain Text
Sitewide sale opens. Elena letter format with personal context: the new arrivals are now in the mix, customers asked about the sets, end of spring. Names three or four specific products in specific colours, hyperlinked text CTA.
Hero callouts: Solene · Game Day Set · Clara Sculpt · New arrivals all eligible
Sitewide discount needed (suggest 20% off, 22-25 May, code TBC)
Why now: April’s only sitewide event was Easter. The MD sale ran on dresses only. Memorial Day is the moment for a sitewide lever. Broader product mix, end-of-month revenue push, and the new arrivals get a discount window in their first 6 days on site.
07
Memorial Day, The Long-Weekend Edit
Memorial Day · Sunday 24 May · Image Editorial
Mid-sale image-led reminder. Hero products and the new arrivals featured visually. Aimed at shoppers who opened Friday’s letter but didn’t click. Closes with "ends midnight Monday."
Solene · Clara Sculpt Bodysuit · Elara Pinafore · Ease Two-Piece · Game Day Set
Sitewide discount · ends 25 May 11:59pm
Why now: Two-day gap from the Friday letter keeps the sale top of mind without fatigue. The image format gives the new arrivals their proper visual moment alongside the proven heroes.
Week Four Memorial Day Close + Review Drive
08
Memorial Day Last Chance
Elena Letter · Monday 25 May · Plain Text
Plain text founder letter. Sale ends tonight at midnight. Names what’s been moving over the weekend with specific colourways. Soft urgency, hyperlinked text CTA, same proven template as the Easter Last Chance and the 10 May MD close.
Top performers from the weekend (live data, but Solene, Clara, and Game Day Set are the most likely callouts)
Sitewide discount · ends tonight 11:59pm
Why now: Three Elena letter closes across May (Apr 30, 10 May, 25 May) is the highest-converting cadence we have evidence for. This is the template that owns the close.
09
A Quick Favour from Gabby
Review Drive · Thursday 28 May · Gabby Letter
Casual letter from Gabby asking recent buyers (post-MD and post-Memorial Day) for a TrustPilot review. Personal tone, no pressure. The thank-you is real money: $50 off your next order for anyone who leaves a verified review.
Audience: anyone who placed an order in the last 60 days. Direct link to TrustPilot. Code delivered post-review or via auto-trigger if Klaviyo + TrustPilot integration supports it.
$50 off your next order · for verified TrustPilot reviews
Why now: Reviews are an open pain point. Two back-to-back sale events have just delivered hundreds of orders, the freshest possible review pool. Gabby is the right voice for this ask. The $50 thank-you is generous enough to actually move people to write the review, which is the bottleneck.
Open Decisions
  • Memorial Day discount level & mechanics. Suggesting 20% off sitewide, 22-25 May. Needs sign-off on % and code.
  • Mother’s Day audience targeting. Recommend tighter segments for May 5 (engaged 60-day) given the Apr 24 broad blast already went to 36k. Confirm the segment shape.
  • Clara Sculpt Bodysuit availability. Site shows several sizes sold out. Confirm full size range will be in stock for the 16 May launch and 19 May spotlight.
  • $50-off review code mechanics. One-time use, minimum spend (if any), expiry, and whether Klaviyo or TrustPilot fires the email after the review is verified.
