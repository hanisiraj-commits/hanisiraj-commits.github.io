Seasonal read
August is peak heat, then a hinge
The first three weeks are the hottest of the American year. Dresses, sets, bare legs. Then the last week turns. College football starts, school goes back, evenings cool off. The calendar follows that shape: dresses and sets until the 21st, pants and layers after it.
A free lever
Tax-free weekends cover the whole catalogue
Texas and Virginia waive sales tax on clothing from the 7th to the 9th. Maryland runs the 9th to the 15th, Florida through the 20th, Connecticut the 16th to the 22nd. The exemption caps at $100 an item, which covers everything Lohnr sells apart from the Mercer Coat and the Aspen Jacket. A saving we don't have to fund.
The Month
August at a glance
Ten sends, built around one weekend. The sale runs Friday the 14th to Monday the 17th and it's the only discount all month. Weeks one and two warm the list with product rather than countdowns. Week four turns towards autumn.
