Seasonal read
August is peak heat, then a hinge
The first three weeks are the hottest of the American year. Dresses, sets, bare legs. Then the last week turns. College football starts Thursday the 27th, main slate the 29th, school goes back, evenings cool. The calendar follows that shape.
New over repeats
Fresh tops and pants, not last month's email
The Getaway pieces went out in late July, so August leads with Capri Button-Up and Sojourn Pant instead. Both landed after that send and neither has had airtime. Same logic across the newer sets and dresses that have never had a feature of their own.
The Month
August at a glance
Twenty sends, built around one weekend. The End of Summer Sale runs Friday the 14th to Monday the 17th and it's the only discount all month. Everything else is a clean single-product feature. Game Day moves up front so it's in hand before kickoff. Send days shift week to week.
