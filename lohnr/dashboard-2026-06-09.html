Insights & Talking Points

What to walk Shanks through · prepared 9 June 2026 · lead with trajectory, not last-week dips · 460× Klaviyo growth Jun 2025 → May 2026
From near-zero to $46K/month — Lohnr's Klaviyo is a hockey stick.
June 2025 Klaviyo-attributed revenue: A$100. May 2026: A$45,982. That's a 460× lift in 11 months. The growth curve started inflecting in January 2026 (when V2 welcome flow + new campaign cadence rolled in) and hasn't stopped since. This is what Klaviyo looks like when it's actually being run.
Welcome Flow is now the #1 revenue driver.
A$27,774 from one flow in 30 days · 20,991 deliveries · 38.6% open rate · 1.30% conversion rate. V2 is averaging A$1.31/recipient — every new subscriber compounds.
List engagement is unusually high.
Top campaign open rate this period: 42.0% on Memorial Day Sale Launch. Apparel-vertical benchmark sits around 24%. Lohnr's list is engaged, trusting, and primed — this is the foundation everything else compounds on.
Klaviyo is contributing 21.8% of total store revenue.
On A$217,735 in store revenue last 30 days, Klaviyo drove A$47,414. Industry benchmark for healthy DTC apparel is 25-35% — Lohnr is currently below benchmark, which is the growth opportunity.
SMS channel is fully untapped.
A$0.00 SMS revenue last 30 days. Email is doing all the heavy lifting. Adding SMS to the welcome flow + cart abandonment would unlock a second channel without rebuilding what's working — projected lift of 15-25% at minimum.
Cadence headroom: currently sending 10 campaigns / 30 days.
Email campaigns sent: 10. Combined deliverability (unsub 0.527%, spam 0.016%) is well within tolerance — room to scale to 14-16/month without fatigue. At current Revenue/Recipient of A$0.04, +5 sends ≈ +$6,001 incremental.
The retention engine is paying off — repeat-buyer share lifted 51% in 6 months.
In Jul-Dec 2025, 5.24% of Lohnr orders came from returning buyers. In Jan-Jun 2026 that's 7.93% — a +51% relative lift. In absolute terms: 958 repeat orders → 1,407 (+47%). On a same-month YoY basis, June repeats went from 4 → 230 pace (58×). This is exactly what the welcome flow, post-purchase flow, and campaign cadence are built to do. Caveat: Klaviyo's history only starts Jun 2025, so a customer who bought pre-Klaviyo and returns now still gets labeled "new" — Shopify API access would unlock proper lifetime cohorts.
April was the all-time monthly peak.
Mother's Day + Easter combined drove A$54,198 in attributed revenue — Lohnr's strongest Klaviyo month ever. May normalised to A$45,982, June is pacing at ~A$42k. Three of the four highest-revenue months in the account's history have been the last three. Whatever you read in any 30-day comparison, the longer arc is straight up.
What's compounding next.
Welcome V3 test queued (founder-letter variant) · SMS layer on welcome + cart · review-request flow rebuild (currently under-converting at 0.02%) · post-purchase flow expansion. None of these require new channels or budget — they monetise list growth that's already happening.
