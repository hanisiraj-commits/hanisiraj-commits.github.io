Three Klaviyo plays for the new associate
Alongside the wider Customer Success proposal, these are the moments our Klaviyo data can flag but email can't close. Automation detects; the associate converts by phone — a personal connection, no written paper trail, far higher conversion. Each runs off the same loop: a trigger fires an internal alert, the associate calls, the outcome is logged.
