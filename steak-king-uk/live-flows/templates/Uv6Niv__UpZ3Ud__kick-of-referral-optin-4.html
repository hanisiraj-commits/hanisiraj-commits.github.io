Quick bit of education. Here’s how what ACTUAL wagyu means

Angus gives structure and proper beefy flavour. Wagyu gives marbling and richness the likes of which British beef can't touch.

The marble score (M) runs 1 to 9+. Standard supermarket steak wouldn't even get scored. An M3 Australian Wagyu ribeye is already miles ahead of anything you've had from Waitrose. An M7 will genuinely change your understanding of what a steak can taste like.

The best steakhouses on earth (London, New York, Tokyo, Sydney) serve Australian Wagyu. Not British. Not American. There's a reason for that, and until now, the reason you couldn't get it in the UK at a sensible price was the middleman problem from the last email.
