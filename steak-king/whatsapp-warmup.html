How WhatsApp tiers work

Quick context so the plan makes sense.

Meta puts every WhatsApp Business account into one of five tiers. The tier caps how many unique users you can message in any 24-hour window. The way you climb is by using at least half your current cap with a High quality rating, after which Meta auto-upgrades you within hours.
