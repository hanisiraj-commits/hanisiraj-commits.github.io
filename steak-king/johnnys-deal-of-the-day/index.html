Johnny's Deal of the Day — series key visual

The locked lockup for every Deal of the Day send, the Deals page and the nav entry. Same mark every time. Only the deal beneath it changes.

Full-width lockup band on solid black. Placement: directly beneath the Steak King header logo, above the deal hero.

Download PNG

What is fixed (never changes)

  • The clock is the letter O in "OF". It is not a sticker and never sits outside the wordmark.
  • "24 HOURS ONLY" lives inside the clock face. The urgency is built into the mark, so no separate urgency badge is needed anywhere else in the email.
  • The clock is ringing — bells on top, motion lines both sides.
  • "JOHNNY'S" in handwritten script above, "DEAL OF THE DAY" in the heavy cream slab beneath, coral rule under the wordmark.
  • Johnny is the face — cut out on black with a coral rim light, tomahawk in hand, overlapping the type so he reads as part of the mark.
  • Solid black background so the band drops into the existing email system and can overlay a hero image.

Where it goes
