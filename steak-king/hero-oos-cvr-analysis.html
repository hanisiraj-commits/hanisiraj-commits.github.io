For David · Hero stock-out check

On the days a hero cut is out of stock,

I marked every normal trading day where a top wagyu hero recorded zero sales (our read that it was out of stock), and compared store CVR on those days to the days everything was stocked. Sale days are excluded, since a sale lifts CVR on its own.

The dip is sharpest over the recent window and fades as you look further back. That's expected: these three cuts only became reliable daily sellers in early April, so before then a zero-sales day often just meant low demand, not a stock-out, which waters the signal down.
