For David · Stock-out impact

When our core cuts sell out,
store conversion follows.

We backfilled the last 90 days, flagged every day a core wagyu cut was out of stock, and split it three ways: normal days, Meat Emporium, and flash sales (dated from the actual campaign sends). On normal trading days, a stock-out cuts store-wide CVR ~7% and revenue per session ~19%. During sales it doesn't show, because the heroes are kept in stock for the sale and the discount traffic masks it.

How to read it. A core cut recording zero sales on a day is our signal it was out of stock. The clean read is Normal days: 12 stock-out days vs 26 fully-stocked, a real dip in both CVR and revenue per session. Meat Emporium heroes were kept in stock throughout (only 1 out-of-stock day in the whole sale window, so there's nothing to compare). During flash sales the discount lift swamps any stock-out effect. Sale windows are dated from the campaign sends. Core cuts: M9+ Ribeye, M9+ Striploin, M4-5 Fillet.
