I marked every normal trading day where a top wagyu hero recorded zero sales (our read that it was out of stock), and compared store CVR on those days to the days everything was stocked. Sale days are excluded, since a sale lifts CVR on its own. Two windows below.

A hero that normally sells every day but records zero units was almost certainly out of stock (Shopify doesn't keep historical stock levels). Heroes tracked: M9+ Ribeye, M9+ Striploin, M4-5 Fillet. The 90-day window starts early April, the point these three became reliable daily sellers, so a zero day genuinely signals a stock-out. Earlier than that they sold only a few units a day and zeros can't be read as stock-outs.
