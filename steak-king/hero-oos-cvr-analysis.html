How much do we rely on our core cuts?

Steak King's day-to-day revenue leans on a handful of core wagyu products. We backfilled the last 90 days to see whether stock-outs of those cuts actually move store-wide sales, or whether the range absorbs it. It doesn't absorb it. Across 15 stock-out days, store conversion ran on average 4.5% lower and revenue per session 13.6% lower than the in-stock days in the same period. Here's the day-by-day.
