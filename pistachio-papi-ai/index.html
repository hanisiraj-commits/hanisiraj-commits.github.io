04 — How it works

The recipe.

One custom model on fal.ai, refined over three rounds on the small things that make Papi look like Papi.

Pipeline

  • Train once: 7 captioned Papi references → FLUX LoRA (~$2, ~6 min). Reusable trigger word.
  • Generate: any scene from a prompt — ~15s, ~5–15¢ each.
  • Select & polish: roll 2–3 candidates for tricky grips, pick the believable one.
  • Two-stage: Kontext drafts with the real jar for an accurate product and natural hands, then the LoRA restyles it into the Papi finish.

The Papi bible (locked)

  • Big aquiline nose · heavy-lidded eyes · furrowed brow
  • Black handlebar moustache, curled at the tips
  • Top-knot man-bun + red tie · gold hoop earring
  • Hand-inked outlines + watercolour cel-shading
  • Exaggerated muscle, anatomically grounded

What the rounds fixed

  • One locked face: same angle and expression every time
  • Real branded jar referenced from the website
  • Hands grip naturally (Kontext + real holding photo)
  • Two-stage render keeps the watercolour finish

To production

  • Founder sign-off on AI Papi at all
  • Designer drops real brand logos into blank zones
  • Retrain on 12–15 hi-res refs for near-exact lock
  • Build a prompt library per campaign type
