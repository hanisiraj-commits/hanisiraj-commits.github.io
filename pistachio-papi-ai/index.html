How it works

The pipeline (all on fal.ai)

  • Training: 7 captioned Papi reference crops → FLUX LoRA fast-training (1,600 steps, ~6 min, ~$2 one-time). Produces a reusable PISTACHIOPAPI trigger word.
  • Generation: FLUX-LoRA inference — any scene from a text prompt, ~15s and ~5–15¢ each.
  • Hero / exact-match shots: FLUX Kontext multi-reference (feed face + body) when a specific pose must match perfectly.

Papi character bible (locked from the source art)

  • Big long aquiline nose · heavy-lidded suave eyes · furrowed brow (3 creases)
  • Thick glossy black handlebar moustache, curled up at the tips
  • Jet-black hair in a high top-knot man-bun with a red hair tie · gold hoop earring
  • Warm tan skin · lean-muscular build · green pistachio-shell wristband
  • Bold hand-inked outlines of varying weight + watercolour cel-shading with brush texture
  • Palette: Papi green #8e9839, dark green #3e4728, cream, black ink, gold accents
