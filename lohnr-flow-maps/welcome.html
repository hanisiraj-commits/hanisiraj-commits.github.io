Image asset brief

What to collect for the Welcome flow, email by email. We’re gathering an asset pool here, not locking a final layout, since none of these emails are designed yet, so we’ve asked generously. Every number is a floor, not a ceiling. Product grids pull dynamically from the store, so no product shots are needed there. E3’s founder portrait is client-supplied, and E5 leans on real UGC we source rather than shoot.

The 2×2 new-arrivals block pulls live from the store, so no product shots are needed there. The hero must read as brand-world, not a product or offer shot.
