Palmora™ 3-Month Roadmap
Email & Lifecycle

A roadmap for the first 3 months.

Two flows per month, list growth running in parallel, a content calendar live by month 3, and a VIP Club ready to launch on the back of it.
Scope

Four workstreams.

A

Pre-purchase flows

Six flows shipped over three months — Welcome, Abandoned Checkout, Abandoned Cart, Browse Abandonment, VIP Welcome, Post-purchase.

B

List growth

Continuous popup A/B testing plus a dedicated VIP landing page in M2 to support Meta-ad opt-in.

C

Content calendar

Monthly campaign calendar built and shipping from M3, running month-by-month.

D

VIP Club

Members-only community for limited drops and early access. Direct opt-in via Meta ads, supported by VIP Welcome flow.

Month-by-Month

The 90-day build.

Month 1

Foundation

Pre-purchase flows

  • Welcome series — fortified series anchored on WELCOME10, sets the brand voice and routes new subscribers
  • Abandoned Checkout

List growth

  • Install Alia popup (or confirm if already live)
  • Run first A/B test on the popup
Month 2

Rollout

Pre-purchase flows

  • Abandoned Cart
  • Browse Abandonment

List growth

  • Second round A/B test on the popup
  • Build full-page landing pages for Meta-ad traffic — direct VIP Club opt-in
Month 3

Engagement + VIP launch

Pre-purchase flows

  • VIP Welcome — for new members entering the VIP Club
  • Post-purchase

Content calendar

  • Build the monthly campaign calendar — running month-by-month from here

VIP Club

  • Launch dedicated Meta ads driving to the VIP landing page built in M2
