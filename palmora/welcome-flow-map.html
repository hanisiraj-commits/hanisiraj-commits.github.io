F_WELCOME — Welcome series
5 emails · 9 days · brand identification + 15% conversion

Welcome flow for Palmora newsletter / popup / "Notify Me When It's Available" subscribers. Single track, no audience splits. E1 sends to all new subscribers; E2–E5 filtered to non-purchasers (0 orders since signup). Modern prestige register — short, sectional, designed. E4 is the deliberate plain-text rhythm break — single-block letter from the founder. This wireframe is the topology reference for the Klaviyo implementation specialist.
